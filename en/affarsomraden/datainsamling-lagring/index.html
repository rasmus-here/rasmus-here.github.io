Data Collection & Storage

We build data bases that make it easier to make decisions, follow up on marketing and manage consent in a safe way.

How we help

Take control of your data and GDPR-secure your business from the ground up.

We always start by understanding the current situation, the business goals and what obstacles stand in the way of the next step. After that, we prioritize activities that can be measured, implemented and followed up together with the right people in your organization.

  • Tracking and data model
  • Consent, GDPR and server-side tracking
  • Data quality and storage structure

A stable foundation for digital growth

Data collection is not just about collecting more data. It's about collecting the right data, in the right way, with the right structure. We help companies create a foundation where marketing, analysis and business follow-up can rest on the same logic.

Data quality, consent and storage

We review how data is collected, which systems are used, how consent is handled and how the information is stored. This makes it easier to work GDPR-consciously and at the same time obtain data that can be used in practice.

Server-side and future-proof tracking

As browsers, platforms and privacy requirements change, tracking needs to become more robust. We help with server-side tracking, data models and implementations that make the measurement more sustainable over time.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about data collection & storage

What is meant by data collection and storage?

It is about how data is collected from website, apps, forms, CRM and advertising platforms and how it is structured, stored and used for analysis.

Can you help with server-side tracking?

Yes. We can evaluate needs, recommend solutions and help with implementation together with the customer's technical environment.

How do you work with GDPR?

We help create technical and organizational conditions for responsible data collection, but legal judgments should always be anchored with legal expertise.

Do we need server-side tracking?

It depends on your channels, data quality and privacy requirements. We evaluate the benefit against the complexity and recommend server-side where it makes the measurement more robust and future-proof.

How do you ensure data quality?

We review how data is collected, structured and stored, reduce duplicates and noise and create a common logic so that market, analysis and business follow-up can rely on the same numbers.
