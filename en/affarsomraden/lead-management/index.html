Lead Management

We help you take care of leads all the way from campaign and form to qualification, sales dialogue and follow-up.

How we help

Secure the processes from traffic source to qualified dialogue and deal.

We always start by understanding the current situation, the business goals and what obstacles stand in the way of the next step. After that, we prioritize activities that can be measured, implemented and followed up together with the right people in your organization.

  • Lead flows and qualification
  • CRM and marketing automation support
  • Sales and market follow-up

From traffic to business opportunity

Lead management is about ensuring that interest from marketing is actually taken forward. We help companies build flows where leads are captured, qualified, prioritized and followed up.

Better interaction between market and sales

When responsibilities, statuses and definitions are unclear, valuable leads are lost. We create structure for what counts as a lead, MQL, SQL and business opportunity and how each step should be handled.

CRM, automation and reporting

We can support with CRM structure, form flows, marketing automation, notifications and dashboards. The goal is for both marketing and sales to see which activities lead to the right type of dialogue.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about lead management

What is lead management?

Lead management is the process of collecting, qualifying, prioritizing and following up leads from first contact to sales dialogue and deal.

Can you connect marketing to CRM?

Yes. We help with forms, tracking, lead sources, statuses and reporting to make CRM data more useful.

How to improve the quality of leads?

Through clearer target groups, better offers, right channel selection, qualification questions and closer follow-up between market and sales.

Which systems do you work with in lead management?

We work with common CRM and marketing automation tools as well as form and tracking solutions, and adapt to your existing environment instead of forcing a system change.

How do you connect marketing and sales?

We define common concepts (lead, MQL, SQL), statuses and responsibilities, and build follow-up so that both functions see which activities lead to business.
