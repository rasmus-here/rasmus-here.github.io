Software Development

We develop customer-unique systems, web apps, integrations and internal tools from initial needs analysis to launch and ongoing management. AI helps us work faster, but architecture, security and business benefit drive every decision.

How we help

We take responsibility for the entire system development life cycle and build operational systems, tools and integrations with modern AI technology.

We always start by understanding the current situation, the business goals and what obstacles stand in the way of the next step. After that, we prioritize activities that can be measured, implemented and followed up together with the right people in your organization.

  • Preliminary study, requirements analysis and system architecture
  • AI-assisted programming, integrations and testing
  • Operation, security, maintenance and further development

System development that solves concrete problems

Many organizations rely on manual processes, fragmented data or systems that do not communicate with each other. We start with the actual needs of the business and build solutions that simplify work, reduce sources of error and create better decision-making bases.

From idea to stable system solution

We can take responsibility for the entire journey: target image, requirements, user flows, technical architecture, data model, implementation, quality assurance and launch. It provides a coherent process where technical choices can be linked to the needs of the users and the long-term management of the solution.

AI as an accelerator in development work

We use modern AI tools for prototyping, code, testing, debugging and documentation. It shortens lead times and frees up more time for business logic, user experience, integrations and quality. All code and technical decisions are still reviewed by humans.

Customer-specific systems that can be further developed

We build internal tools, web apps, integration layers, dashboards, automated workflows and digital customer services, among other things. The solutions receive a clear structure and documentation so that they can be managed, scaled and further developed over time.

Our process

From need to managed system solution

We work through the entire system development life cycle. Each phase has a clear purpose, concrete deliveries and the possibility of ongoing feedback.

  1. 01

    Feasibility study & requirements analysis

    We map the business's needs, users, goals, data sources and technical conditions. The result is prioritized requirements, a clear scope and a common picture of what the system must achieve.

  2. 02

    Design & architecture

    We plan user flows, interfaces, system structure, integrations, databases and security principles. The architecture is adapted for both today's delivery and future further development.

  3. 03

    Programming & implementation

    The system is built iteratively from scratch or by adapting existing platforms and APIs. AI-assisted tools are used where they provide faster development without compromising quality.

  4. 04

    Testing & quality assurance

    We test functionality, user flows, integrations, permissions, performance and relevant security requirements. Deviations are corrected and the solution is verified against the agreed requirements before launch.

  5. 05

    Operation & maintenance

    We help with launch, monitoring, documentation, security updates and ongoing improvements. The system can then be managed by you, by us or in a joint team.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about software development

What does system development mean?

System development is the entire process of planning, designing, building, testing, launching and managing a digital system. The work thus encompasses more than programming and begins with understanding the actual needs of the business and users.

What types of systems can HERE build?

We build internal business tools, web apps, integrations, dashboards, automations, form flows and customer-unique digital services. The solution can be standalone or linked to, for example, CRM, business systems, data sources and market platforms.

What does your process for system development look like?

We work through five interrelated phases: feasibility study and requirements analysis, design and architecture, programming and implementation, testing and quality assurance, and operation and maintenance. The work takes place iteratively so that you can follow, test and influence the solution along the way.

How do you use AI in system development?

AI is used to support prototypes, code generation, test cases, troubleshooting and documentation, among other things. Human review, architecture, security assessments and business understanding still drive the solution and final decisions.

Can you build on existing systems?

Yes. We can analyze existing code, APIs, databases and data sources to develop new features or integrations without replacing the entire system environment. The feasibility study shows which parts can be reused and where modernization provides the greatest benefit.

How do you work with quality and safety?

We set requirements for permissions, data processing, error handling, logging and testing based on the solution's risk level. Code and dependencies are reviewed, sensitive data is restricted and security measures are followed up even after launch.

How quickly can you deliver a system?

A limited prototype or an internal tool can often be developed in a few weeks, while larger systems with multiple integrations require more time. After the feasibility study, we provide a roadmap with stages, priorities and clear deliverables.

Do we own the code and the solution?

Yes. Solutions we build are documented and handed over according to the plan we agree on. You must be able to manage and further develop the system yourself, with us or together with another technical partner.
