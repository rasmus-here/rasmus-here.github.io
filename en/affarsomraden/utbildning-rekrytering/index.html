Training & Recruitment

We train teams and support organizations that want to build stronger internal capabilities in digital growth.

How we help

Develop the team's capabilities and find the right digital skills for the next step.

We always start by understanding the current situation, the business goals and what obstacles stand in the way of the next step. After that, we prioritize activities that can be measured, implemented and followed up together with the right people in your organization.

  • Workshops and training programs
  • Competence mapping
  • Support in recruiting digital roles

Build digital competence internally

As digital marketing, data and AI change rapidly, organizations need to be able to develop their own capabilities. We train teams so that more people understand the channels, measurement and decisions behind digital growth.

Workshops based on reality

Our courses can be adapted to your goals, tools and challenges. It can be about SEO, Google Ads, GA4, reporting, content, lead management or how marketing and sales should work together.

Support in recruitment

We also help define roles, requirement profiles and skill needs when companies hire digital specialists. It reduces the risk of recruiting errors and makes onboarding more focused.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about training & recruitment

What courses does HERE offer?

We offer workshops and training in digital marketing, SEO, advertising, tracking, reporting, lead management and digital strategy.

Can the training be adapted to our team?

Yes. We adapt the level, content and examples to the organization's goals, tools, roles and existing knowledge.

How can you support recruitment?

We can help with requirements profile, interview questions, skills assessment and advice on what type of digital role the organization needs.

Where and how are the courses conducted?

We hold training courses and workshops at your place or digitally, adapted to the team's level and everyday life. The content is based on your tools, goals and challenges.

How do we know what skills the team needs?

We carry out a skills mapping that highlights strengths and gaps in the team and recommends whether the next step is training, reinforcement or recruitment.
