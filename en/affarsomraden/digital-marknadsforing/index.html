Digital Marketing

We plan, produce, optimize and follow up digital marketing with a focus on business benefit, not just clicks and views. The work connects channel strategy, content, media, tracking and ongoing analysis.

How we help

Get more out of SEO, Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn and content with senior specialists.

We always start by understanding the current situation, the business goals and what obstacles stand in the way of the next step. After that, we prioritize activities that can be measured, implemented and followed up together with the right people in your organization.

  • SEO and content
  • Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads and Snapchat Ads
  • Conversion optimization and ongoing analysis

Digital marketing with the business at the center

Good digital marketing starts with the business objective. That's why we work with target groups, margins, buying journeys and measurement before we scale up the budget. The result is channels that work together and can be followed up in a way that management, market and sales understand.

From strategy to operational optimization

HERE can take responsibility for strategy, channel selection, campaign structure, ad texts, content, landing pages and reporting. We work closely with the client's team and prioritize what has the greatest potential to generate relevant traffic, more leads, better sales or a stronger brand position.

Measurement that makes work better

We connect digital marketing to GA4, Google Tag Manager, CRM, advertising platforms and dashboards. In this way, it becomes easier to understand which channels create demand, which efforts convert and where the next improvement should be made.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about digital marketing

Which channels does HERE work with in digital marketing?

We work with SEO, Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, TikTok Ads, Snapchat Ads, content strategy and conversion optimization. The choice of channel is governed by the target group, business goals and measurable potential.

Can you take over existing advertising accounts?

Yes. We can conduct a review of existing accounts, set a new structure and then take responsibility for ongoing optimization, budget prioritization and reporting.

How do you measure the effect of digital marketing?

We set up measurement plans, conversion tracking and reporting based on the business goal. The focus is on leads, revenue, ROAS, pipeline, cost per result and quality in traffic.

How quickly do we see results?

Paid channels such as Google Ads and Meta Ads can provide measurable traffic and leads within days, while SEO and content build impact over several months. We set milestones early and prioritize the channels that can most quickly prove business value before scaling the budget.

How much does it cost to hire HERE for digital marketing?

The structure is adapted according to ambition, number of channels and how much operational responsibility we take. After an initial meeting, we propose a limited scope with a transparent price, what is included and how the work is followed up.

Can you work together with our existing marketing department?

Yes. We can take overall responsibility, strengthen an individual channel or coach your internal team. Roles and decision paths are set from the start so that the knowledge stays with you.
