SEO that supports the business

We focus on keywords and content that have business potential, not just high volume. The work is linked to target groups, offers and conversion points so that organic traffic can contribute to leads, sales or a clearer brand position.

AI-EO and GEO for next generation search

As more users search via AI tools, summary search results and generative answers, content needs to be clear, credible and easy to interpret. We help companies structure expert content, FAQs, concepts, comparisons and sourced answers so that the brand has a better chance of being picked up by AI search.

Technology, content and authority

A strong SEO foundation requires the website to be crawlable, understandable and usable. We analyze technical obstacles, structure, metadata, content gaps, internal linking, schema markup and how the page can become more relevant to both users, search engines and AI systems.

Ongoing SEO work

SEO is long-term work where insights, prioritization and follow-up need to take place continuously. We help with roadmap, production, optimization and reporting so that the improvements do not stop at an audit.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about SEO

How long does SEO take?

SEO is long-term and the effect depends on competition, current situation and effort. Technical improvements can have a quick impact, while content and authority are often built over months.

What are AI-EO and GEO?

AI-EO and GEO are about optimizing content for AI-powered search experiences and generative responses. The focus is on clear answers, credible structure, expertise, sources, schema markup and content that AI systems can more easily understand.

Can you help us appear in AI search?

Yes. We help companies create content, FAQs, topic clusters and technical structure that increase the possibility of being picked up in AI search and generative search results.

Do you work with technical SEO?

Yes. We analyze indexing, structure, loading, metadata, canonicals, internal links, schema markup and technical barriers that affect visibility.

Can you help with SEO content?

Yes. We develop keyword strategy, content plans, briefs and optimized texts based on target group, business goals and how search behavior changes with AI.

How much does SEO at HERE cost?

SEO work is usually set up as an ongoing task where the scope is controlled by competition, current situation and ambition. After an initial analysis, we propose a prioritized plan with a transparent price and clear measurement points.
