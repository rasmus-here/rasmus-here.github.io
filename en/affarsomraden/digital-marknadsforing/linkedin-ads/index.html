FAQ

Frequently asked questions about LinkedIn Ads

When are LinkedIn Ads best suited?

LinkedIn is particularly suitable for B2B, recruitment, employer branding, events and campaigns where the target group can be defined professionally.

Are LinkedIn Ads expensive?

The cost per click is often higher than in many other channels, but the value can be strong when the target audience is narrow and the business value per lead is high.

Can you connect LinkedIn leads to CRM?

Yes. We can support with lead forms, UTM structure, CRM flows and reporting.

How does a startup with LinkedIn Ads work?

We define target group based on role, industry and company, choose campaign type according to goal and package content that motivates the more expensive clicks, with forms and CRM connection for follow-up.

How do you measure the results of LinkedIn Ads?

For B2B, we focus on lead quality and pipeline rather than volume. We connect campaigns to forms, UTM and CRM to see which target groups actually create dialogue and business.
