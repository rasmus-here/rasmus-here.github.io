FAQ

Frequently asked questions about TikTok Ads

Are TikTok Ads suitable for all businesses?

No. The channel is best suited when the target group is there and when the company can work with creative material that feels relevant to the format.

Do you need a lot of video?

Video is central to TikTok, but the most important thing is to work with clear creative hypotheses and continuous testing.

Can you set up the TikTok Pixel?

Yes. We can help with pixels, events, UTM structure and reporting.

How does a startup with TikTok Ads work?

We secure pixel, event and conversion goals, develop creative hypotheses adapted to the format and build a test structure so that the account learns before the budget is scaled.

How do you measure the results of TikTok Ads?

With pixels, events and UTM structure, we track cost per result and website behavior after clicks, and evaluate the role of the channel in the entire media mix.
