Privacy Policy

Here we describe how Hither AB, which operates HERE, processes personal data when you visit the website, contact us or start a business relationship with us.

Last updated: 15 July 2026

Personal data controller

Hither AB, organization number 559033-9353, is the personal data controller for the processing described in this policy. You can reach us via info@here.se or at Nääs Fabriker, Spinnerivägen 1, 448 50 Tollered.

Contact HERE about personal data

What data we process

We mainly process data that you yourself provide to us and limit the processing to what is needed for the current purpose.

  • Name, email address, phone number, company and the content of your request.
  • Data that arise in customer dialogue, quote work, contracts and ongoing deliveries.
  • Technical information that may appear in server logs or embedded services, such as IP address, device, browser and time.

Purpose and legal basis

We use the data to respond to inquiries, assess and provide quotes, administer customer relationships, deliver contracted services, fulfill legal obligations and protect our systems. The processing is based, depending on the situation, on measures before or fulfilling an agreement, legal obligation or our legitimate interest in conducting and developing the business.

Suppliers and recipients

We only share personal data when necessary. Suppliers who process data on our behalf must do so in accordance with our instructions and applicable data protection requirements.

  • Netlify is used for the operation and delivery of the website.
  • The contact form is provided as an embedded service from kitsu.se.
  • Google Maps is used to show our offices and can receive technical information when the maps are loaded.
  • Accounting, IT and other business providers can access data when required for their assignments.

Transfer outside the EU and EEA

Some suppliers may process data outside the EU or EEA. When this happens, the transfer must be supported by a decision on an adequate level of protection or other approved protection measures, for example the European Commission's standard contract clauses.

How long the data is saved

We save personal data as long as it is needed for the purpose. Inquiries that do not lead to a customer relationship are normally thinned no later than 24 months after the last contact. Data relating to contracts, deliveries or accounting are saved for the time required to fulfill the contract, handle legal claims and comply with legal requirements.

Your rights

You may have the right to receive information about the processing, request access to or correction of data, request deletion or restriction, object to processing and in some cases obtain data in a portable format. Contact info@here.se to exercise a right. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swedish Data Protection Authority.

Read about your rights at IMY

Security and updates

We take technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, loss and destruction. The policy may be updated when the website, our services or the rules change. The current update date is displayed at the top of the page.
