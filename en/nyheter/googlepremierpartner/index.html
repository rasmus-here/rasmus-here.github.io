HERE belongs to the top 3% of Sweden's agencies within Google Ads

HERE has once again been named a Google Premier Partner. You can read about the benefits here.

By HERE Editors

In this article

A seal of quality within Google AdsWhat it means for our customersStrategy before clicks
01

A seal of quality within Google Ads

Google Premier Partner status is given to agencies that meet high standards for competence, results and customer development in Google Ads. For HERE, it is proof that our ongoing work is of a high standard.

02

What it means for our customers

As a Premier Partner, we get access to insights, training and support that help us stay abreast of developments in Google's advertising platforms. This makes it easier to make better decisions regarding budget, account structure and optimization.

03

Strategy before clicks

Our work with Google Ads does not start in the ad account. We connect business goals, margins, audiences and tracking before scaling up. That's how advertising becomes a tool for profitable growth.
