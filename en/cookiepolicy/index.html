Cookie policy

Here we explain what cookies are and how the website here.se uses its own technology and embedded services.

Last updated: 15 July 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that can be stored on your device when you visit a website. They may be necessary for a function, remember settings or be used for statistics and marketing. Similar technology can also read information from your device.

Our current usage

With the latest update, here.se's own web application does not use its own analysis or marketing cookies. If we introduce such technology, we will update this policy and obtain consent before non-essential storage or access occurs.

External and embedded services

The website contains features from other providers. When they are loaded, the provider can process technical information and use cookies according to its own terms.

  • Kitsu/Markstack: the embedded contact form sends the information you choose to provide via kitsu.se.
  • Google Maps: embedded maps in the footer show our offices and may process IP address, device and browser information, among other things.
  • Netlify: used to deliver the website and may process technical log data for operation and security.

Consent and necessary cookies

Cookies that are strictly necessary for a service that you explicitly request can be used without consent. For other cookies, as a general rule, active consent is required which must be able to be revoked. Continued use of the website does not in itself constitute consent.

Read PTS guidance on cookies

How to manage cookies

You can delete or block cookies in your browser settings. If you block all storage, embedded features such as maps or forms may work differently. The settings are managed separately in each browser and on each device.

Questions and updates

Contact info@here.se if you have questions about the website's use of cookies or similar technology. The policy is updated when the website features or providers change.
